AKAN NAMING SYSTEM

The Akan people of Ghana name their children after the days of the week that they were born,depending on their gender.Listed below are the days of the week and names given to them for both genders;

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Aku
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
